Introducing, The COVID-19 Info Bot!

A bot that provides daily updates and fast and reliable answers to your questions regarding COVID-19!

This bot gives you a convenient way to stay updated about COVID-19 as you no longer need to switch apps as much for COVID-19 info while communicating on Discord!

Commands

/help

To view our list of commands and to find out more about this bot!

/world

Shows you the COVID-19 situation globally!

/cases <continent>

Shows you the number of cases, deaths and recovery in your continent!

/cases <country>

Shows you the number of cases, deaths and recovery in your country!

/symptoms

To view a list of COVID-19 symptoms ranging from common, uncommon and rare!

/news

To receive links to several useful news outlets regarding COVID-19!

/resources

To receive links to several informative COVID-19 resources! Ex) Travel restrictions

/preventions

To learn how to protect yourself and others from COVID-19!

/vote

To vote or review The COVID-19 Info Bot!
